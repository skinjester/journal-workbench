Senior UX Designer, Connect | Sony Interactive Entertainment 

I’m drawn to the Senior UX Designer, Connect role because it centers on the parts of the player experience where clarity, continuity, and trust matter most. That has always been a through line in my career.

My strongest work focuses on connected ecosystems where account, commerce, support, and platform constraints all shape the customer experience. What matters to me in that work is not just reducing friction, but respecting the situation a person is in, whether they are setting something up for the first time or trying to recover something important. I’m comfortable working in ambiguous, technically complex spaces, partnering across disciplines, and bringing structure to systems that span multiple surfaces and stakeholders.
