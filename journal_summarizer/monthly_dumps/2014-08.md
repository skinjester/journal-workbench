## [2014-08-05 23:16] eso

quickslot assignment really is more straightforward when you must push the stick in the direction of the slot you want to assign to and then press confirm

here's how it works
-	the player can exit the quickslot UI at any time, whether an assignment has been made or not
-	assigning an item to a quickslot from an inventory list places it into a pocket (a temporary storage area) at the center of the quickslot wheel
-	the attached tooltip panel always shows what the joystick is focused on
-	when the stick is at rest, focus is on the pocket in the center of the wheel
-	pushing the stick focuses on the slots in the quickslot wheel
-	focusing on a quickslot and CONFIRMING can have the following outcomes
	-	if nothing was in the pocket, the item in the quickslot is transferred to the pocket
	-	if nothing was in the quickslot, the item in the pocket is transferred to the quickslot
	-	if nothing was in either the pocket or the quickslot, then nothing happens
	-	if both the pocket and the quickslot are occupied then these items are swapped
