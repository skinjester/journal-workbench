## [2015-05-18 11:24] eso

lots of design in motion right now

Craft Bags Acceptance Criteria

Plus members will have an account shared craft bag that holds an enormous amount of crafting materials so that they no longer compete with regular items for inventory space.

-	Players can access the craft bag from any character (Account Shared)

-	Crafting materials that are looted or gathered automatically go into the craft bag

-	No effort should be on the player to utilize the bag
	-	It is automatically used when crafting
	-	The player can easily interact with items in the craft bag for activities such as  trade or to be posted to a guild store

-	When the player cancels their sub
	-	Items can still come out of the bag but cannot go in.
	-	The bag can be sorted and filtered based on crafting profession

-	The system supports selling this as a permanent unlock
	-	PD Note: by default this will be a subscriber only bonus, but it should be developed in such a way as to be convertible to a purchased unlock as well
	-	The craft bag appears for plus members and non-plus members but is only accessible to plus
	-	Non-plus members are informed of how the bag works and how to get it if they attempt to use the craft bag.
	-	A link to account management and plus purchase is provided to the player
