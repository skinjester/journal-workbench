### PART 1 — What the role is really asking for

#### The explicit asks

- **Program-level senior IC design** on the Community team: streamer-side creation flows and viewer-side engagement, across Twitch’s ecosystem—not a narrow single-widget role.
- **Cross-functional execution** with PMs, engineers, and other designers; hands-on research collaboration; design reviews and critique culture.
- **5+ years** UX with a **documented emphasis on content discovery and/or content engagement** (this is the primary resume filter after title/level).
- **Interaction + visual craft**, including **prototyping mobile experiences** (expect mobile mocks, flows, and possibly ship-adjacent mobile work samples).
- **Influence on strategy and roadmaps**—not only executing tickets.
- **Mentorship** of junior designers and propagation of patterns/process.
- **Figma** listed as a hard “You Have.”

#### The implied asks

- Comfort with **high-ambiguity consumer social/UGC** product surfaces, metrics-heavy iteration, and strong opinions from creators and viewers.
- Ability to **represent design upward** (exec-facing narrative) and **sideways** (senior partners in product and engineering).
- Fluency in **qualitative + quantitative** reasoning (the JD calls this out explicitly).
- Operational resilience: Amazon/Twitch-scale constraints, accessibility, policy, and performance are rarely spelled out but show up in practice.

#### What "great" looks like for this role

- Someone who can **name discovery and engagement problems** in creator/viewer language, tie them to measurable outcomes, and show **systems thinking** (patterns, consistency, scalable IA) without drowning the product in generic design-system talk.
- A portfolio that makes **mobile and cross-platform** fluency obvious quickly.
- Evidence of **shipping or near-shipping** iterative work in a **live** environment (even if not Twitch).

#### What a hiring team is optimizing for in screening

- **Keyword fit**: “content discovery,” “engagement,” “mobile,” “Figma,” “mentor,” “cross-functional,” “strategy.”
- **Level calibration**: senior scope, not a mid-level who needs constant direction.
- **Domain credibility**: UGC/live/community experience is a bonus, not the gate—but absence of *any* adjacent narrative hurts when the bar is 5+ years in that problem space.

#### Likely doubts unless proven otherwise

- **Industry frame**: A career heavy in **game UX and embedded consulting** reads as adjacent but not automatic fit for **web/mobile streaming product** unless reframed hard.
- **Discovery/engagement proof**: The JD wants explicit experience; game UI and commerce are related, but screeners may still ask “where’s Twitch/YouTube/Instagram/Reddit?”
- **Mobile**: Your strongest public work emphasizes **console/controller and desktop**; mobile prototyping must be demonstrated, not assumed.
- **Recency**: Contract studio branding can raise “how current is platform product org work?” unless timelines and outcomes are crisp.
